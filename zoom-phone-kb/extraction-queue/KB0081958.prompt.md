# Frequently asked questions about purchasing hardware with Zoom Phone

Zoom is introducing an improved online experience that lets customers easily select and purchase desk phone hardware directly through our self-serve buying process. Self-serve hardware purchase options are available directly in the Zoom Phone pricing page, checkout, and order confirmation screen.

## Frequently asked questions about purchasing hardware with Zoom Phone

### Can I purchase hardware without buying a Zoom Phone license?

No. Hardware can only be purchased with a new license or as part of a bundle within the United States only.

### What happens if I forget to assign my device after purchase?

You’ll receive reminders via email and the Billing Portal. Assign the device to a user to complete setup.

### What if I submit a tax-exemption certificate after purchasing hardware?

Tax information submitted within 30 days of purchase will be reviewed by our tax teams.

### Who do I contact for hardware technical support?

Please reach out to [UC Direct](https://zoom.ucdirect.com/help) for all technical hardware support.

### Will I get tracking info for my hardware order?

Yes. You’ll receive a confirmation email from UC Direct with tracking details, and the information will appear in your Billing Portal.

### What happens if my hardware order is denied?

UC Direct has the right to reject orders. Zoom can review any cases regarding software orders if applicable.

### Can I return or get a refund for hardware?

Yes, if the box is unopened and returned within 30 days using UC Direct's [RMA form](https://zoom.ucdirect.com/rma). Learn more about [UC's direct return policy](https://zoom.ucdirect.com/return-policy).

### Can I use a P.O. box as my shipping address?

No. Only valid U.S. street addresses are accepted for hardware shipments.

### What is the device purchase ceiling for online customers?

There is a maximum of 50 devices per order, enforced at checkout. This is designed to filter out large/enterprise orders.

### What happens if a customer changes their shipping address post-purchase?

Zoom informs customers that UC Direct manages all post-purchase order changes. The customer should reach out to the [UC Direct help form](https://zoom.ucdirect.com/help) for assistance.
