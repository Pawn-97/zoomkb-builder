# Transferring call data from Zoom Phone to Contact Center

Account owners and admins can pass selected information from Zoom Phone when transferring phone calls to Contact Center. All transferred calls will include the caller name/ID of both the original caller and the transfer initiator.

Learn more about [transferring voice call data from Contact Center to Zoom Phone](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0078153).

### Requirements for transferring call data from Zoom Phone to Contact Center

  * Account owner or admin privileges
  * Either a Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, or a standalone Zoom Phone calling plan



## Table of Contents

  * How to enable or disable transferring call data to Contact Center



## How to enable or disable transferring call data to Contact Center

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit account settings.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **Phone System Management** then **Company Info**.
  3. At the top of the page, click **Account Settings**.
  4. Under **Preference** , locate the **Transferring call data to Contact Center** setting.
  5. Select the **Conversation summary** checkbox to enable transfer to Zoom Contact Center. Deselect the checkbox to disable transfer to Zoom Contact Center.
