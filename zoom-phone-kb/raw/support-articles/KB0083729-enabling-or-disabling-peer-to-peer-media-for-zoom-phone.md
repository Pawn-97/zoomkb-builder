# Enabling or disabling Peer-to-Peer media for Zoom Phone

Account owners and admins can allow Zoom clients and supported desk phones to send media directly to each other. This setting requires the Zoom clients and supported desk phones to be restarted for changes to take effect.

**Note** : This feature will be available to all customers in phases over the next few months.

Learn more about [using Peer-to-Peer media](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0083727) and [supported desk phones for peer-to-peer media](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0061373#h_44760422141321765494255324).

### Requirements for enabling or disabling Peer-to-Peer media for Zoom Phone

  * Either a Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, or a standalone Zoom Phone calling plan
  * Account owner or admin privileges



### Limitations for Peer-to-Peer media for Zoom Phone

Peer-to-Peer media for Zoom Phone has the following limitations:

  * This setting is only available with Zoom Workplace app version 6.7.0 or later.
  * Users or devices that have certain features like recording or monitoring enabled may not be able to use Peer-to-Peer media.

## How to enable or disable Peer-to-Peer media for Zoom Phone

### Account

To enable or disable **Peer to Peer Media** for all users in the account:

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit account settings.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **Account Management** then **Account Settings**.
  3. Click the **Zoom Phone** tab.
  4. Under **General** , click the **Peer to Peer Media** toggle to enable or disable it.
  5. If a verification dialog appears, click **Enable** or **Disable** to verify the change.
  6. (Optional) To prevent all users in your account from changing this setting, click the lock icon , and then click **Lock** to confirm the setting.



### Group

To enable or disable **Peer to Peer Media** for a group of users:

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit groups.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **User Management** then **Groups**.
  3. Click the applicable group name from the list.
  4. Click the **Zoom Phone** tab.
  5. Under **General** , click the **Peer to Peer Media** toggle to enable or disable it.
  6. If a verification dialog appears, click **Enable** or **Disable** to verify the change.  
**Note** : If the option is grayed out, it has been locked at the account level and needs to be changed at that level.
  7. (Optional) To prevent all users in the group from changing this setting, click the lock icon , and then click **Lock** to confirm the setting.



### Site

To enable or disable **Peer to Peer Media** for all site users:

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit account settings.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **Phone System Management** then **Company Info**.
  3. Click the applicable site name from the list.
  4. Click the **Policy** tab.
  5. Under **General** , click the **Peer to Peer Media** toggle to enable or disable it.
  6. If a verification dialog appears, click **Enable** or **Disable** to verify the change.
  7. (Optional) To prevent all users in your site from changing this setting, click the lock icon , and then click **Lock** to confirm the setting.



### Phone user

To enable or disable **Peer to Peer Media** for a phone user:

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit account settings.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **Phone System Management** then **Users & Rooms**.
  3. Click the **Users** tab.
  4. Click the applicable user name from the list.
  5. Click the **Policy** tab.
  6. Under **General** , click the **Peer to Peer Media** toggle to enable or disable it.
  7. If a verification dialog appears, click **Enable** or **Disable** to verify the change.  
**Note** : If the option is grayed out, it has been locked at either the group or account level. You need to contact your Zoom admin.
