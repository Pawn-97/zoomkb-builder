# Enabling or disabling Zoom Phone conference join/leave tone

Account owners and admins can disable conference join tones at the account level for [Zoom Phone conferences](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0059185). When users join conferences, tones will no longer play if this setting is disabled.

The feature is enabled by default, allowing admins to maintain the current experience or customize it based on their organization's preferences. This enhancement provides greater control over the audio experience during conference calls.

**Note** : This setting does not affect the [conference-join options configured for shared line appearances](https://support.zoom.com/hc/en/article?id=zm_kb&sysparm_article=KB0067483#mcetoc_1hv5sol6soi).

### Requirements for enabling or disabling Zoom Phone conference join/leave tone

  * Either a Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, or a standalone Zoom Phone calling plan
  * Account owner or admin privileges

## How to enable or disable Zoom Phone conference join/leave tone

### Account

To enable or disable **Conference join/leave tone** for all users in the account:

  1. Sign in to the Zoom web portal as an admin with the privilege to edit account settings.
  2. In the navigation menu, click **Account Management** then **Account Settings**.
  3. Click the **Zoom Phone** tab.
  4. Under **General** , click the **Conference join/leave tone** toggle to enable or disable it.
  5. If a verification dialog appears, click **Enable** or **Disable** to verify the change.
