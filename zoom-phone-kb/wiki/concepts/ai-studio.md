# AI Studio

## Summary

The admin interface for managing AI-generated content templates, accessed through AI Companion > AI Studio > Custom Templates > Call Summary Templates. This is the central hub where admins create, edit, test, publish, and manage templates. The navigation path (under AI Companion rather than Phone settings) could be unexpected for admins focused on phone features.

## Key points

- Accessible via AI Companion > AI Studio > Custom Templates > Call Summary Templates
- Central hub for all template lifecycle actions: create, edit, customize, test, publish, manage
- Not under Phone settings -- located in the AI Companion admin area
