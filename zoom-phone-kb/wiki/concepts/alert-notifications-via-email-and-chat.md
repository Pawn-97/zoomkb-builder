# Alert Notifications via Email and Chat

## Summary

Each alert can be configured with up to 10 email recipients and up to 3 chat channel integrations. A frequency and time-frame setting controls when and how often notifications are sent. The combination of email and chat channels means notifications may arrive in multiple places, requiring consistent message formatting across channels.

## Key points

- Up to 10 email recipients per alert with acknowledgement checkbox
- Up to 3 chat channel integrations (requires verification token and endpoint)
- Configurable notification frequency and time frame, including a 24-hour continuous option
