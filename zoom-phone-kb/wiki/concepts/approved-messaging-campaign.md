# Approved Messaging Campaign

## Summary

A prerequisite registration for phone numbers used in SMS messaging, required before a number can send SMS through Zoom Phone. The article notes that if a campaign does not exist, one must be created. This introduces a potentially hidden dependency in the setup flow that could block users who are unaware of the requirement.

## Key points

- Required for any phone number used for SMS in Zoom Phone
- Must be created before the SMS toggle can function
- Referenced as a prerequisite rather than a step in the main flow
- Potential hidden blocker for admins setting up SMS for the first time
