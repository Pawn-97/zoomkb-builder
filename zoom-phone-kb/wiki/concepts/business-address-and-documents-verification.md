# Business Address and Documents Verification

## Summary

A prerequisite flow accessed via a Related Features dropdown on the Phone Numbers page. Admins add a business address, upload required documents, and wait for verification. The UI must make it clear that this step is blocking number provisioning and provide status visibility during the review process.

## Key points

- Requires verified business address in the same country as target phone numbers
- Involves document upload (company verification, tax residence proof)
- Verification status is displayed as 'Verified' when complete
- Admins receive email notifications at review start and completion
- Step 1: Sign in to Zoom web portal as an admin
- Step 2: Navigate to Number Management > Phone Numbers
- Step 3: Open Related Features dropdown and select Business Address & Documents
- Step 4: Click Add Address and complete all required fields
- Step 5: Upload required documentation if prompted
- Admins receive email when review starts and when complete
