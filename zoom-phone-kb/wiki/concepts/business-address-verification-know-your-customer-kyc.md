# Business Address Verification (Know Your Customer / KYC)

## Summary

A mandatory identity and address verification process required before provisioning or porting UK business mobile numbers in Zoom Phone Mobile. The verification involves submitting business documentation, proof of identity, and proof of address through the admin portal. Designers must treat this as a gating step that blocks number-related actions until completed, with clear status communication during review.

## Key points

- Mandatory for all UK mobile number porting and provisioning requests
- Known as Know Your Customer (KYC) verification
- Requires legal business name, UK address, proof of business entity, proof of identity, and proof of address
- A single verification can be used for multiple number orders
- Verification status must be communicated clearly since it gates all subsequent actions
