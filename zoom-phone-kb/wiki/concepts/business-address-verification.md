# Business Address Verification

## Summary

A prerequisite for moving native phone numbers between accounts: the destination account must have a relevant business address created in a verified state. BYOC numbers are exempt from this requirement.

## Key points

- Required only for native numbers, not BYOC
- Address must be verified before the transfer can proceed
- Acts as a gate in the move workflow: admin must select business address from a dropdown
