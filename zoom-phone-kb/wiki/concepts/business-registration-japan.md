# Business Registration (Japan)

## Summary

The first phase of Japan SMS registration where organizations register their legal business information (prefecture, legal company name, corporate number, website, legal form) with Japanese mobile carriers. Unlike SMS registration, business registration has no separate approval -- instead, the information is validated during SMS registration approval. This deferred validation model means errors surface late in the process, so the UX should help catch mismatches early.

## Key points

- Required prerequisite for SMS registration
- No separate approval process; validated during SMS registration review
- Includes: prefecture, legal company name, department name, corporate number, website, legal form
- Corporate number is verified against legal company name by mobile carriers
- Can be updated at any time unless associated SMS registration is in pending or active status
