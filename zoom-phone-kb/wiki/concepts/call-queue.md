# Call Queue

## Summary

A Zoom Phone construct that manages incoming call routing to a group of members. Call queues support multiple distribution methods (including group rotating and simultaneous) and overflow behaviors for unanswered calls.

## Key points

- Accessible under Phone System Management > Call Queues
- Configured via a Profile tab with Business Hours section
- Members are added to the queue and calls are distributed per the chosen method
