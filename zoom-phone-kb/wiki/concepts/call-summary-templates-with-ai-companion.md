# Call Summary Templates with AI Companion

## Summary

A feature that allows account owners and admins to create and manage custom templates for AI-generated call summaries, integrated with Zoom's Custom AI Companion. Templates define the format, sections, and terminology used in post-call summaries, enabling organizations to standardize call documentation for industry requirements, compliance, or business processes. The feature supports both predefined templates for common call types and custom templates generated via natural language prompts.

## Key points

- Integrates with Custom AI Companion to generate formatted call summaries
- Supports predefined templates (General, One-on-one, Project update, Discussion, Customer success) and custom templates created from scratch via AI prompts
- Custom dictionaries improve transcription accuracy across all Zoom Phone communications (summaries, transcripts, voicemail, recordings)
- Templates can be assigned to specific users or groups with Custom AI Companion licenses
- Must be enabled before call summary templates can be used
- Works in conjunction with custom dictionaries for improved transcription accuracy
- Available under the AI Companion section of Zoom Phone settings
