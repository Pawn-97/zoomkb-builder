# Caller Data Enrichment

## Summary

The enriched caller context provided to Contact Center agents when receiving transferred calls from Zoom Phone users, queues, auto receptionists, or shared line groups. This information follows the engagement lifecycle and is designed to give agents progressively deeper context as the interaction evolves.

## Key points

- Information appears in three stages: incoming notification, active engagement, and completed engagement records
- Includes consumer identity, transfer source details, IVR input, conversation summaries, and CRM ticket info
- Helps agents provide informed support without requiring the caller to repeat context
