# Certified Devices

## Summary

A curated list of desk phone models that Zoom officially supports for Phone features. Designers should treat certified devices as the 'happy path' in device management flows, while any model not on this list falls into the non-certified category with warning UI.

## Key points

- Includes specific models from AudioCodes, Avaya, Cisco, Grandstream, Mitel, Poly, and Yealink
- Certified devices benefit from automatic model detection and correction
- All other desk phone models are treated as a general uncertified category
