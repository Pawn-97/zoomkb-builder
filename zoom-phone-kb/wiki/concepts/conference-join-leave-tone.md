# Conference Join/Leave Tone

## Summary

An audio tone that plays when users join or leave a Zoom Phone conference. Enabled by default, it provides an audible awareness cue for conference participants. Designers should consider the audio UX implications of this default-on behavior — the tone signals presence changes but may be disruptive in certain organizational contexts.

## Key points

- Audible tone played when a participant joins or leaves a Zoom Phone conference
- Enabled by default for all users in the account
- Configurable only at the account level, not per group/site/user
- Does not affect shared line appearance conference-join options
