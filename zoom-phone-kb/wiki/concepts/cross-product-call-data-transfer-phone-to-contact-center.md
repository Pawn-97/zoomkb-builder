# Cross-Product Call Data Transfer (Phone to Contact Center)

## Summary

A feature that passes call information from Zoom Phone to Zoom Contact Center when calls are transferred between the two products. Transferred calls carry the caller name/ID of both the original caller and the transfer initiator, preserving context across product boundaries. This is a one-directional setting (Phone to Contact Center) with a separate companion feature for the reverse direction.

## Key points

- Transfers caller name/ID of both the original caller and the transfer initiator
- One-directional: Phone to Contact Center (reverse transfer uses a different article/setting)
- Controlled by a single account-level checkbox labeled 'Conversation summary'
- Requires account owner or admin privileges to configure
