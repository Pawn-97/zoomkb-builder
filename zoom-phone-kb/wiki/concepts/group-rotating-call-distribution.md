# Group Rotating Call Distribution

## Summary

A call distribution method that rings subsets of call queue members in sequence rather than all members simultaneously. Each group of a defined size is rung for a set duration; if unanswered, the call moves to the next group. The pattern repeats until all members are exhausted, then falls through to overflow settings.

## Key points

- Configurable group member size and ringing duration per group
- Sequential group ringing: group 1, then group 2, etc., until all members tried
- Calls overflow to voicemail, forwarding, or another queue if all groups fail
- Distinct from simultaneous ringing where all members ring at once
