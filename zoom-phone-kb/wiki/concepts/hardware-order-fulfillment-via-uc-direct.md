# Hardware Order Fulfillment via UC Direct

## Summary

Zoom partners with UC Direct for hardware order fulfillment, technical support, returns, and post-purchase order changes. This third-party relationship means the customer journey splits after purchase: Zoom handles the software/license while UC Direct handles all physical hardware logistics. Designers must clearly communicate this handoff to avoid customer confusion about who to contact.

## Key points

- UC Direct manages hardware shipping, tracking, technical support, and returns
- Customers receive order tracking from UC Direct via email and the Zoom Billing Portal
- Post-purchase address changes must be handled through UC Direct, not Zoom
- UC Direct has the right to reject orders independently of Zoom
