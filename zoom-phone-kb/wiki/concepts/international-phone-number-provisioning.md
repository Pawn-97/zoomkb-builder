# International Phone Number Provisioning

## Summary

The process of acquiring phone numbers for specific countries through Zoom Phone's Provider Exchange portal. This is a compliance-heavy workflow requiring verified business addresses and documentation, and represents a high-friction experience where regulatory gates directly shape the UI flow.

## Key points

- Phone numbers must be provisioned per-country with a matching verified business address
- Uses Provider Exchange portal for number selection and purchase
- Selection controls are deactivated until verification prerequisites are met
- Some countries (e.g., Spain, Australia) require additional documentation beyond basic address verification
