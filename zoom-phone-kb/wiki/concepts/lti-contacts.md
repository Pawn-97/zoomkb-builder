# LTI Contacts

## Summary

The course roster displayed within LTI Pro with the ability to attach phone numbers to individual students. Once populated, contacts become click-to-dial or click-to-SMS targets. Designers should treat this as an editable directory that bridges LMS roster data with Zoom Phone communication.

## Key points

- Course roster pre-populated from LMS, initially without phone numbers
- Inline editing to add phone numbers per contact
- Dedicated Phone and SMS action icons per contact row
- Phone numbers expire after 180 days
