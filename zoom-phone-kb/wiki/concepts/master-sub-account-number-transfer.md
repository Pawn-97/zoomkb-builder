# Master-Sub Account Number Transfer

## Summary

Allows admins to move phone numbers between related master and sub-accounts without support tickets. All number types (native, BYOC) are supported across Zoom Phone and Zoom Contact Center products. Numbers must be in an unassigned state before moving, and native numbers require a verified business address at the destination.

## Key points

- Supports individual, bulk, and CSV import transfer methods
- Native numbers require verified business address at destination account
- BYOC numbers can move freely without address validation
- Numbers must be unassigned before they can be transferred
- Destination account must have appropriate licenses (Zoom Phone or Zoom Contact Center)
