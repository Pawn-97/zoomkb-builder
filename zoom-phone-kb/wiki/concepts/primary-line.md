# Primary Line

## Summary

The higher-tier Zoom Phone Mobile license type providing 50GB of high-speed data per billing cycle with up to 20GB of mobile hotspot usage. After data limits are reached, speeds are throttled: general data to 500 kbps, hotspot to 128 kbps. Resets each billing cycle.

## Key points

- 50GB high-speed data monthly
- Up to 20GB high-speed mobile hotspot included
- Post-limit throttling: 500 kbps general, 128 kbps hotspot
- Must be configured as primary SIM on device
