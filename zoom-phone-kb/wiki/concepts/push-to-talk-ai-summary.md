# Push to Talk AI Summary

## Summary

An AI Companion feature that generates structured summaries of push-to-talk phone calls, including time period, quick recap, next steps, and full summary sections. The summary is available post-call in call history logs and can be edited and emailed, making it both a real-time catch-up tool and a persistent reference.

## Key points

- Generates summaries with timed sections: Summary time period, Quick recap, Next steps, Full summary
- Available both during the call (catch-up) and after the call via call history
- Summaries can be edited by the user and emailed
- Requires account-level enablement of Push to Talk AI summary and email report
