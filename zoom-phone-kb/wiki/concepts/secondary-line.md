# Secondary Line

## Summary

The lower-tier Zoom Phone Mobile license type providing 2GB of high-speed data per billing cycle with no mobile hotspot capability. After 2GB, general data speeds are reduced to 500 kbps. Suitable for lighter usage scenarios.

## Key points

- 2GB high-speed data monthly
- No mobile hotspot available
- Post-limit: 500 kbps for remainder of billing cycle
- Must be configured as secondary SIM on device
