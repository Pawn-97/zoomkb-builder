# Self-Serve Hardware Purchase for Zoom Phone

## Summary

An online purchase flow that lets customers buy desk phone hardware directly through Zoom's self-service pricing page, checkout, and order confirmation screens. This embeds hardware sales into the software purchasing journey, turning what was likely a separate sales-assisted process into a self-guided ecommerce experience.

## Key points

- Hardware options appear on the Zoom Phone pricing page, checkout, and order confirmation screen
- Customers self-select and purchase hardware without contacting sales
- Hardware can only be bought alongside a new Zoom Phone license or as a bundle (US only)
