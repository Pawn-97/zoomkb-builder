# Temporary Numbers in Zoom Phone Mobile

## Summary

Interim phone numbers assigned to users while business address verification is in progress or while waiting for permanent number porting. These numbers allow users to begin using Zoom Phone Mobile service without waiting for full verification. The UX must clearly differentiate temporary from permanent numbers and manage the transition when verification completes.

## Key points

- Bridges the gap during verification or porting wait times
- Accessible from Number Management after verification is submitted
- Can be assigned to users to enable immediate service usage
- Users need to understand these are temporary and will be replaced
