# Transferring Call Data Policy

## Summary

A configuration that controls what caller information is shared when transferring calls from Zoom Phone to Zoom Contact Center, and vice versa. The policy defines which data fields accompany a transferred call -- including original caller info, transfer initiator details, conversation summaries, and CRM data -- giving admins granular control over the information available to receiving agents.

## Key points

- Configurable independently for both Zoom Phone and Zoom Contact Center directions
- Caller name and caller ID are always included in all transferred calls (non-optional)
- By default, all available data is shared with Zoom Contact Center
- Phone side controls conversation summary sharing; Contact Center side controls original caller info, transfer initiator details, consumer sentiment, CRM ticket info, variables, and address book custom fields
