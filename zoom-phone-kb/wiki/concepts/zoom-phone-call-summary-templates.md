# Zoom Phone Call Summary Templates

## Summary

AI Companion-powered templates that structure how call summaries are formatted and presented to users. Designers should consider that templates can be predefined (system-provided) or custom (admin-created), each offering different levels of flexibility in how call intelligence is surfaced.

## Key points

- Admins can create custom templates to match business-specific formatting needs
- Templates apply across call summaries, transcripts, voicemail transcriptions, and recordings
- Predefined and custom template types coexist, with admins selecting which to apply
- Setting can be configured at multiple hierarchy levels: account, group, site, phone user, and personal
