# Zoom Phone eSIM

## Summary

A virtual SIM that allows users to activate their Zoom Phone business number directly on a compatible smartphone's native dialer without a physical SIM card. Designers should consider how activation flows differ from traditional phone setup and ensure clear guidance for users transitioning between personal and business lines on a single device.

## Key points

- eSIM is a digital SIM provisioned remotely by admins, eliminating physical SIM handling
- Activation supports two paths: QR code scanning via camera app and manual code entry via device settings
- Once activated, the Zoom Phone number integrates with the phone's native dialer for calling while advanced features remain in the Zoom app
- Receives activation email with QR code and manual codes after admin issues eSIM
- Can access activation message through web portal (Phone > Settings > eSIM > View QR Code) as an alternative to email
- Can generate new QR codes themselves when upgrading devices (without losing the old device)
