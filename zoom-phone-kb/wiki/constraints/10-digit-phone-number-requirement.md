# 10-Digit Phone Number Requirement

## Summary

Only 10-digit phone numbers can be assigned to the auto receptionist for SMS functionality. Numbers with other digit counts are not supported. The UX should filter or validate number selections to prevent assignment of ineligible numbers.

## Key points

- Assigned phone number must be 10 digits
- Numbers with other digit lengths are not supported for SMS
- Should be validated at the number selection step
