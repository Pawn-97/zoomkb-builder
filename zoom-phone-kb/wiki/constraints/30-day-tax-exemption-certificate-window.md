# 30-Day Tax Exemption Certificate Window

## Summary

Tax exemption certificates submitted within 30 days of purchase are reviewed by Zoom's tax team. Certificates submitted after 30 days are presumably not accepted, though the article does not explicitly state the consequence. The post-purchase UI should surface this window and provide a submission mechanism.

## Key points

- Tax exemption certificates must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
- Reviewed by Zoom's internal tax team (not automated)
- Implicit cutoff at 30 days
