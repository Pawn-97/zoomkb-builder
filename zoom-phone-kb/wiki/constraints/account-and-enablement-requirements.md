# Account and Enablement Requirements

## Summary

The feature requires a Pro, Business, or Enterprise Zoom Workplace account and both the Push to Talk AI summary and email report settings enabled by an admin. Designers should plan for graceful degradation when prerequisites are not met, with clear messaging about missing requirements.

## Key points

- Requires Zoom Workplace Pro, Business, or Enterprise account
- Push to Talk AI summary must be enabled at account level
- Push to Talk AI summary email report must also be enabled
- Requires Zoom mobile app for Android or iOS at minimum global version or higher
- AI Companion may not be available for select verticals and regional customers
