# Automatic Correction Limited to Certified Models

## Summary

The automatic model detection and correction system only applies to the specifically listed certified device models. Designers should not assume this feature works for all devices; non-certified models follow a different (warning-only) path.

## Key points

- Only corrects listed certified models from AudioCodes, Avaya, Cisco, Grandstream, Mitel, Poly, and Yealink
- Non-certified devices or uncategorized models receive warnings only, no automatic correction
- The list of certified models is fixed and versioned per manufacturer
