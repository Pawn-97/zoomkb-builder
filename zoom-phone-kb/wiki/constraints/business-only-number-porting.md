# Business-Only Number Porting

## Summary

Only business mobile numbers can be ported to Zoom Phone Mobile UK. Personal mobile number porting is not supported. This restriction means the UX should help users distinguish between business and personal lines and prevent attempts to port ineligible numbers.

## Key points

- Porting is only available for business mobile numbers
- Personal mobile number porting is not supported
- PAC code must be obtained from the business mobile provider
- Users may not know whether their number is classified as business or personal
