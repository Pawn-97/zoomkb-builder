# Business Registration Lock During SMS Review

## Summary

Business registration information cannot be updated when an associated SMS registration is in pending or active status. If carriers reject the SMS registration due to business info inconsistencies, the admin must wait for rejection before making corrections. This lock-then-fix workflow can extend timelines and should be clearly explained.

## Key points

- Business registration is locked from editing when associated SMS registration is pending or active
- Updates are only possible when no linked SMS registration is in pending/active state
- Rejected SMS registrations may require business info corrections before resubmission
