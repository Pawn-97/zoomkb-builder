# Custom AI Companion Add-On Required

## Summary

The Custom AI Companion add-on is a prerequisite for call summary templates. Without it, the feature is unavailable. This license gate should be handled gracefully in the UI, with clear upgrade paths for admins who discover the feature but lack the required add-on.

## Key points

- Custom AI Companion add-on is required
- Call summary with AI Companion must be enabled first
- Call summary templates must be enabled separately (KB0084831)
- Required alongside a Zoom Workplace or standalone Zoom Phone license
- Enables custom template creation and custom dictionary features
- Feature availability may be hidden or disabled without this add-on
