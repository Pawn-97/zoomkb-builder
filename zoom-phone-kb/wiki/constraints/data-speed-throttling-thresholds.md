# Data Speed Throttling Thresholds

## Summary

Both Primary and Secondary lines have hard data caps after which speeds are significantly throttled. Primary: 50GB general / 20GB hotspot. Secondary: 2GB general / no hotspot. Throttled speeds (500 kbps general, 128 kbps hotspot) persist until the billing cycle resets.

## Key points

- Primary: 50GB cap, throttles to 500 kbps (data) / 128 kbps (hotspot)
- Secondary: 2GB cap, throttles to 500 kbps, no hotspot available
- Resets at billing cycle boundary
- Design implication: usage meter and threshold warnings in admin/end-user views
