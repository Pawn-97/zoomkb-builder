# Device Compatibility and Carrier Lock

## Summary

Users must have a compatible eSIM-enabled smartphone on iOS or Android. The device must not be carrier-locked; locked devices will prevent eSIM installation and activation.

## Key points

- eSIM-capable smartphone required (iOS and Android supported)
- Carrier-locked devices are incompatible
- Design implication: pre-activation compatibility check should flag locked devices
