# Document Freshness (3-Month Rule)

## Summary

All uploaded documents must be dated within the last 3 months. This means returning customers may need to re-upload documents even if previously verified. The UX should warn users when documents are approaching or past the 3-month threshold to avoid rejection.

## Key points

- All documents must be dated within the last 3 months
- Proof of address (utility bill) has a secondary limit of 6 months
- Previously verified documents may expire and require re-upload
- Clear date-validation feedback is needed during upload
