# Email Matching Requirement for Authorization

## Summary

The Zoom user account email must be identical to the email used to log into the LMS. A mismatch silently blocks authorization with a login failure. Designers should provide clear, specific error messaging when email mismatch is detected.

## Key points

- Zoom account email must match LMS login email exactly
- Mismatch results in a confusing login failure if not clearly surfaced
