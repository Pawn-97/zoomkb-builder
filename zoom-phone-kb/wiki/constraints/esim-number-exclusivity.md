# eSIM Number Exclusivity

## Summary

Zoom Phone Mobile numbers can only be assigned to Zoom Phone users and cannot be used by other products. This exclusivity constraint affects how designers model the relationship between eSIM numbers and user accounts in admin interfaces.

## Key points

- eSIM numbers are exclusive to Zoom Phone users
- Cannot be shared with or used by other Zoom products
