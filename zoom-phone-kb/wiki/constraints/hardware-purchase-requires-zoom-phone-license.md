# Hardware Purchase Requires Zoom Phone License

## Summary

Hardware cannot be purchased independently; it must be bought alongside a new Zoom Phone license or as part of a bundle. This bundling constraint means the hardware selection UI should only appear when a qualifying license is in the cart, and should be hidden or disabled otherwise.

## Key points

- No standalone hardware purchase—must accompany a new license or bundle
- Enforced at the pricing page and checkout level
