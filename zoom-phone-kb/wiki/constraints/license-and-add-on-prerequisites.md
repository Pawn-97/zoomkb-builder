# License and Add-on Prerequisites

## Summary

Three prerequisites must be met: a Zoom Workplace license with Phone (or standalone Zoom Phone plan), the Custom AI Companion add-on, and call summary with AI Companion enabled. The UX must handle the case where one or more prerequisites are missing, with clear messaging about what is needed and how to obtain it.

## Key points

- Zoom Workplace with Phone license OR standalone Zoom Phone calling plan
- Custom AI Companion add-on required
- Call summary with AI Companion must be enabled first
- Missing prerequisites should surface contextual upgrade paths
