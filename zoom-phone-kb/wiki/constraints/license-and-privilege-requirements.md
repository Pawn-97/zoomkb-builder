# License and Privilege Requirements

## Summary

Both Zoom Phone and Zoom Contact Center licenses are required, and only account owners or admins with edit privileges can configure the policy. This dual-product requirement means the feature spans across product boundaries, which affects admin navigation and error messaging.

## Key points

- Zoom Phone license (or qualifying Zoom Workplace plan) required
- Zoom Contact Center license required
- Account owner or admin with edit privileges required
