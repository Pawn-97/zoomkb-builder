# License Requirements

## Summary

A valid license is required to access this feature: either a Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, or a standalone Zoom Phone calling plan. Without the correct license, the setting may be hidden or non-functional, and the UI should handle this gracefully.

## Key points

- Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, OR standalone Zoom Phone calling plan required
- Licenseless accounts should see the setting as disabled or hidden with appropriate messaging
- Requires Zoom Workplace license with Phone included, or standalone Zoom Phone calling plan
