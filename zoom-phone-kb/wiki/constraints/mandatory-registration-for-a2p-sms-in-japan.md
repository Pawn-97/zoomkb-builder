# Mandatory Registration for A2P SMS in Japan

## Summary

Both business registration and SMS registration are mandatory before A2P SMS can be used on Japanese phone numbers. There is no bypass or short-circuit path, which means the full registration flow is a hard gate for any organization wanting to use SMS in Japan.

## Key points

- Both registrations are mandatory -- no SMS capability without completing both
- Business registration is a prerequisite for SMS registration
