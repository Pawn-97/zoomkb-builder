# Maximum 50 Devices Per Order

## Summary

A hard limit of 50 devices per order is enforced at checkout to filter out large enterprise purchases that should go through a different sales channel. The quantity selector should display this limit and provide guidance for enterprise customers who exceed it.

## Key points

- 50-device maximum per single order
- Enforced at checkout—quantities above 50 should be rejected or redirected to enterprise sales
- Designed to keep self-serve focused on SMB purchases
