# Maximum Limits for Targets and Recipients

## Summary

Alerts have hard limits: up to 5 devices per 'Specify Device' target, up to 10 email recipients, and up to 3 chat channels. These limits should be enforced in the form with clear counter indicators and disable the add button when the limit is reached.

## Key points

- Maximum 5 devices per 'Specify Device' target
- Maximum 10 email recipients per alert
- Maximum 3 chat channels per alert
