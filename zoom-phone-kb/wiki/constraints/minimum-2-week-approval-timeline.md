# Minimum 2-Week Approval Timeline

## Summary

The full process including external carrier approval takes a minimum of 2 weeks. Zoom recommends starting early to align with implementation timelines. This timeline constraint should be prominently surfaced at the start of the flow to manage expectations and prevent last-minute registration attempts.

## Key points

- Full process takes a minimum of 2 weeks
- External carrier approval is the primary time driver
- Zoom recommends starting as early as possible
