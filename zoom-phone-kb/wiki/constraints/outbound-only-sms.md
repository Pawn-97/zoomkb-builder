# Outbound-Only SMS

## Summary

SMS capabilities granted through Japan SMS registration support outbound messaging only (from business numbers to consumers). Inbound SMS from consumers to the business number is not supported. This directional limitation must be communicated clearly to set accurate expectations.

## Key points

- SMS is outbound only: from registered numbers to end consumers
- Inbound SMS from consumers is not supported
