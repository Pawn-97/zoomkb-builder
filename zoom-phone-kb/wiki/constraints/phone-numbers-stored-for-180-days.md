# Phone Numbers Stored for 180 Days

## Summary

Phone numbers in the LTI course contacts list expire after 180 days. This means instructors may need to re-enter or re-import contact numbers each semester. Designers should consider surfacing expiration warnings and providing a re-import affordance.

## Key points

- 180-day retention on contact phone numbers
- Impacts semester-to-semester workflows where courses are resubscribed
