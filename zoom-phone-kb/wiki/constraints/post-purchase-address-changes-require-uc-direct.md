# Post-Purchase Address Changes Require UC Direct

## Summary

Customers who need to change their shipping address after purchase must contact UC Direct directly. Zoom does not handle post-purchase order modifications. This handoff should be clearly communicated to customers, and the post-purchase UI should provide direct links or contact information for UC Direct.

## Key points

- Shipping address changes after purchase are not handled by Zoom
- Customers must contact UC Direct help form for any post-purchase order changes
