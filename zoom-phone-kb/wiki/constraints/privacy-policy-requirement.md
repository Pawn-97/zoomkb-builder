# Privacy Policy Requirement

## Summary

Organizations must have a compliant privacy policy that states they do not share or sell customers' mobile information with third parties. This is a prerequisite for registration and should be validated early in the flow.

## Key points

- Must have a privacy policy stating no sharing/selling of customer mobile information with third parties
