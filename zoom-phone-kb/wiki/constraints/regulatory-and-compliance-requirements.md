# Regulatory and Compliance Requirements

## Summary

International phone number provisioning is governed by telecom regulations (KYC, emergency services compliance) and anti-fraud measures. These requirements are non-negotiable and cannot be bypassed — without a verified business address in the target country, the system will not allow provisioning regardless of license status.

## Key points

- Verified business address in the same country as the target phone number is mandatory
- KYC procedures require specific documentation (ID, proof of address)
- Emergency services compliance mandates verified physical addresses
- Anti-fraud measures require valid identification regardless of physical location
- Regulatory requirements cannot be bypassed — no workaround exists
