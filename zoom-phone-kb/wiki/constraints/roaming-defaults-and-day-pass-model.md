# Roaming Defaults and Day-Pass Model

## Summary

International roaming is deactivated by default for all users to prevent bill shock. Roaming operates on a day-pass model with two tiers: included roaming locations at no extra cost, and day-pass locations that incur per-day charges.

## Key points

- Deactivated by default—admin must opt in per user
- Day-pass only activates when user actually roams, not at purchase time
- Two tiers: included roaming (free) and day-pass roaming (paid per day)
