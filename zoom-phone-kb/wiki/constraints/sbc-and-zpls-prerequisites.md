# SBC and ZPLS Prerequisites

## Summary

SBC integration requires Multiple Sites enabled for Zoom Phone, a deployed ZPLS service, and the SBC must meet general Zoom BYOC requirements. The SBC also needs DigiCert certificates for TLS connectivity. These compound prerequisites mean the setup wizard should validate readiness before allowing configuration.

## Key points

- Multiple Sites must be enabled for Zoom Phone
- ZPLS service module must be deployed
- SBC must meet general BYOC requirements and have DigiCert certificates
