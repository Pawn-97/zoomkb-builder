# Sub-Account Deletion Number Loss

## Summary

If an admin deletes a sub-account without first moving its numbers back to the master account, the numbers are permanently deleted and released from the Zoom system. The admin has full responsibility to move numbers before deletion.

## Key points

- Design implication: warn or block sub-account deletion when numbers still exist
- No automatic recovery of numbers after sub-account deletion
- Numbers released back to Zoom, not recoverable
