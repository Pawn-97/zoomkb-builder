# UK Business Address Requirement

## Summary

Only UK business addresses are accepted for Zoom Phone Mobile UK eSIM verification. Worldwide addresses are explicitly rejected. The UX must validate the address country early in the flow to prevent wasted effort on ineligible submissions.

## Key points

- Must be a valid business address in the United Kingdom
- Worldwide addresses are not accepted
- Validation of address country should occur early in the workflow
