# US-Only Hardware Purchases with Street Address Requirement

## Summary

Hardware purchasing is only available to customers in the United States, and shipments require a valid US street address (P.O. boxes are not accepted). The checkout form must validate US address formats and reject P.O. box entries with a clear error message.

## Key points

- Geographically restricted to the United States only
- Valid US street address required for shipping—P.O. boxes are rejected
- Address validation should occur at checkout before order submission
