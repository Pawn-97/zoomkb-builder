# Access Alerts and Notifications for ZPA Devices

## Summary

Admins navigate through the Zoom web portal to reach the alert configuration interface. The path involves two levels of navigation (Account Management > Alerts & Notifications) and a tab selection (Zoom Devices), which may benefit from deep-linking for frequent access.

## Key points

- Sign in to Zoom web portal as an admin with edit privileges
- Navigate to Account Management > Alerts & Notifications
- Click the Zoom Devices tab
