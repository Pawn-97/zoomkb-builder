# Access Call Summary Templates

## Summary

Admins navigate to the template management interface through AI Companion > AI Studio > Custom Templates > Call Summary Templates. The four-level navigation depth signals that this is a specialized admin area, but designers should ensure breadcrumbs or persistent navigation help admins maintain orientation.

## Key points

- Sign in as account owner or admin
- Navigate to AI Companion > AI Studio > Custom Templates tab > Call Summary Templates tab
- This is the entry point for all template operations
