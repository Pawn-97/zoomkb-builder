# Access SMS Registration in Web Portal

## Summary

Admins navigate to the SMS setup area and add Japan as a country/region to begin the registration process. This entry point is the gateway to both business and SMS registration, and should clearly indicate the overall process overview and estimated timeline upfront.

## Key points

- Navigate to Number Management > SMS Setup
- Click 'Add country/region' and select Japan
- Serves as the entry point for both business and SMS registration
