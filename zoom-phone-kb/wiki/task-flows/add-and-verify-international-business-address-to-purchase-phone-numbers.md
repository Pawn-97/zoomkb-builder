# Add and Verify International Business Address to Purchase Phone Numbers

## Summary

After completing initial verification, admins add country-specific business addresses, confirm verification status, and proceed to search and select phone numbers. The flow ties address verification status directly to the availability of number selection controls — designers must ensure the relationship is clear to admins.

## Key points

- Step 1: Complete business address and documents verification
- Step 2: Verify that Verification Status shows Verified on the Business Address page
- Step 3: Navigate to Number Management > Phone Numbers > Add Number > Get Number
- Step 4: Select Product, Country/Region, Type, State, and Area Code
- Step 5: Click Search and select desired numbers
- Some countries require additional documentation before numbers can be assigned
