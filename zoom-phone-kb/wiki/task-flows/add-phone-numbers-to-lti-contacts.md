# Add Phone Numbers to LTI Contacts

## Summary

Instructors populate phone numbers for their course roster by selecting a contact and manually adding the number. Each contact row then exposes Phone and SMS action icons for one-click communication.

## Key points

- Open Contacts tab to view the course roster
- Click Edit on a contact row
- Enter phone number and click Add
- Use Phone icon or SMS icon next to the contact to initiate communication
