# Authorize Zoom Account When Creating a Zap

## Summary

Within the Zap creation flow, users must connect their Zoom account to Zapier. They select Zoom Phone as a trigger or action, choose 'Connect a new account', sign in to Zoom, review permissions, and click Allow. This authenticates the specific Zoom account for use in that Zap.

## Key points

- In Zap editor: select Zoom Phone as trigger or action
- Choose 'Connect a new account' in the Account tab
- Zoom OAuth sign-in and permission grant
- Per-Zap connection model: account is connected within the context of a specific Zap
