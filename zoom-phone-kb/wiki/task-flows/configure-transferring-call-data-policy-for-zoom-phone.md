# Configure Transferring Call Data Policy for Zoom Phone

## Summary

Admins enable or disable conversation summary sharing on the Zoom Phone side. The setting is a simple checkbox under Phone System Management > Company Info > Account Settings, with an immediate Save to apply. The simplicity of a single toggle belies the downstream impact on agent experience, so designers should consider adding preview or description text explaining what data appears for agents.

## Key points

- Navigate to Phone System Management > Company Info > Account Settings > Settings tab
- Under Preferences, toggle 'Conversation summary' checkbox in the Transferring Call Data to Contact Center setting
- Click Save to apply changes immediately
