# Create Business Registration

## Summary

Admins register their organization's legal and business details with Japanese carriers. The form collects prefecture, legal company name, department name, corporate number, website, and legal form. The corporate number-to-company-name verification happens later during SMS registration approval, so inline validation should be as thorough as possible at entry time to prevent late-stage rejections.

## Key points

- Enter prefecture, legal company name, department name, corporate number, website, and legal form
- Corporate number will be verified against legal company name during SMS registration approval
- Business registration has no separate approval but information is validated later by carriers
- Can be updated/deleted anytime unless associated SMS registration is pending or active
