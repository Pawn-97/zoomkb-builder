# Create SMS Registration

## Summary

After business registration, admins create an SMS registration by passing through a checklist gate -- they must open and review the Japan SMS registration checklist, then confirm they have read and understood it before the registration form is revealed. This gated pattern forces attention to requirements but adds friction; designers should ensure the checklist is concise and actionable.

## Key points

- Navigate to Japan SMS registration area within SMS setup
- Checklist Requirement page appears with a link to the Japan SMS registration creation checklist
- User must open and view the checklist link before continuing
- Checkbox confirmation ('I confirm that I have read and understood...') unlocks the registration details section
- After confirmation, registration details form appears for completion
