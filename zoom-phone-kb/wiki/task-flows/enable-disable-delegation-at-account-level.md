# Enable/Disable Delegation at Account Level

## Summary

The top-level control for delegation that affects all users in the account. When locked, this setting becomes the single source of truth, and all lower levels are forced to comply. Admins access this through Account Management > Account Settings > Zoom Phone, emphasizing that delegation is a phone-specific feature.

## Key points

- Navigate to Account Management > Account Settings > Zoom Phone tab
- Toggle Delegation under the General section
- Confirm change via verification dialog
- Optional: lock to prevent group/site/user level changes
