# Enable or Disable Call Summary Templates at Phone User Level

## Summary

Admins configure the setting for an individual phone user via Phone System Management > Users & Rooms > [user] > Policy > AI Companion. As the most granular setting level, designers should consider how admins efficiently manage individual overrides alongside group and site defaults.

## Key points

- Path: Phone System Management > Users & Rooms > Users > [user] > Policy > AI Companion
- Grayed out if locked at account level
- Changes apply only to the selected phone user
