# Enable or Disable Conference Join/Leave Tone at Account Level

## Summary

A straightforward single-level configuration accessed through Account Settings. The simplicity of this flow (no group, site, or user variants) means the toggle and confirmation dialog bear the full responsibility for communicating the scope and impact of the change.

## Key points

- Step 1: Sign in to Zoom web portal as an admin with account settings privileges
- Step 2: Navigate to Account Management > Account Settings > Zoom Phone tab
- Step 3: Under General, toggle Conference join/leave tone on or off
- Step 4: Confirm via verification dialog (Enable or Disable)
