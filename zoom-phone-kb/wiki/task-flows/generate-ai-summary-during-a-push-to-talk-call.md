# Generate AI Summary During a Push-to-Talk Call

## Summary

A mobile-first flow where the user selects an audio source, taps the AI Companion icon, chooses a catch-up time option, and receives a structured summary. Designers should ensure the icon is easily discoverable in the call UI and the time options are clear and meaningful.

## Key points

- Step 1: Use push-to-talk on Zoom Phone
- Step 2: Optionally select audio source (Bluetooth, Speaker, Mute, Headphones) from top-left
- Step 3: Tap the AI Companion icon in the top-right corner
- Step 4: Select a catch-up time option from the presented choices
- AI Companion generates summary with time period, quick recap, next steps, and full summary
