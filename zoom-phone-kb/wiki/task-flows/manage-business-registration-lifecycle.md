# Manage Business Registration Lifecycle

## Summary

Admins can update or delete business registrations, but these actions are restricted when an associated SMS registration is in pending or active status. If an SMS registration is rejected, admins may need to update the business registration with corrected information. This state-dependent availability of actions requires clear UI feedback about why certain options are disabled.

## Key points

- Business registrations are accessible within Japan via SMS setup
- Can be updated or deleted unless linked to pending or active SMS registration
- Rejected SMS registrations may require business registration updates based on the rejection reason
