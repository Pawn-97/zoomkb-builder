# Manage Temporary Numbers After Verification Submission

## Summary

After submitting verification, admins can access temporary numbers to assign to users while waiting for approval. The flow involves accessing the temporary numbers list, viewing available numbers, and assigning them to specific users. Designers should ensure the transition from verification submission to temporary number management feels like a natural continuation rather than a disconnected workflow.

## Key points

- Temporary numbers become accessible after verification is submitted
- Admins view available numbers and assign to users
- Bridges the waiting period during document review
- Transition to permanent numbers must be smooth when verification is approved
