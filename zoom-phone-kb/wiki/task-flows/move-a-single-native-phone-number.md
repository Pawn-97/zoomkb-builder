# Move a Single Native Phone Number

## Summary

Admins transfer a single native number from one account to another through a guided pop-up wizard. The flow requires selecting the destination account, product type, subscription, division, SIP group, and verifying the business address before confirming.

## Key points

- Access via ellipsis menu on the Phone Numbers list page
- Five dropdown fields to fill: account, product, subscription, division, SIP group
- Business address selection is an additional required step for native numbers
- Two-step wizard: configuration first (Next), then address verification (Move)
