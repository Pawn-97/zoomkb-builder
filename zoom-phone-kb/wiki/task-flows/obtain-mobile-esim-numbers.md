# Obtain Mobile eSIM Numbers

## Summary

A two-step process where admins first create or verify a business address, then obtain mobile eSIM numbers (new or port-in). Verified business address is a gate: eSIM numbers cannot be obtained without one.

## Key points

- Step 1: Create or verify business address
- Step 2: Obtain mobile eSIM numbers (new acquisition or port-in)
- Business address verification is a prerequisite gate
