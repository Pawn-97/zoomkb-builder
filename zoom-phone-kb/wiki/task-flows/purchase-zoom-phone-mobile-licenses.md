# Purchase Zoom Phone Mobile Licenses

## Summary

Admins acquire Zoom Phone Mobile licenses (Primary or Secondary line types) for their organization. The purchasing flow distinguishes between UK-specific license variants (Primary with premium data, Secondary with essential data).

## Key points

- License type selection: Primary line or Secondary line
- Regional variants exist (e.g., UK-specific SKUs)
- Per-user licensing model
