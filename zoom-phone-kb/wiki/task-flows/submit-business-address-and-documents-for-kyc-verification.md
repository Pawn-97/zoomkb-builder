# Submit Business Address and Documents for KYC Verification

## Summary

Admins navigate to Number Management > Phone Numbers > Related Features > Business Address & Documents, then complete a multi-section form including product selection, country, number type, business information, and document uploads. The flow involves a three-level dropdown cascade (Product > Country > Number Type) to scope the verification correctly, and multiple document upload fields each with format and size constraints that must be validated inline.

## Key points

- Path: Number Management > Phone Numbers > Related Features > Business Address & Documents > Add Address
- Three-level dropdown cascade: Product (Phone) > Country (United Kingdom) > Number Type (Mobile - eSIM)
- Business information section: address, legal business name, registration info
- Three document uploads: Proof of Business, Proof of Identity (passport only), Proof of Address
- Accepted formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF, DOC, DOCX; max 10MB per file
- Documents must be clear, legible, dated within last 3 months, and show complete information
