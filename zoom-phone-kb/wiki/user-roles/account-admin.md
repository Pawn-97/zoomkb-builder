# Account Admin

## Summary

Responsible for enabling Push to Talk AI summary and the associated email report at the account level. The admin's configuration determines whether the AI Companion icon and summary features are available to end users.

## Key points

- Enables Push to Talk AI summary at account level
- Enables Push to Talk AI summary email report
- Must have a Zoom Workplace Pro, Business, or Enterprise account
