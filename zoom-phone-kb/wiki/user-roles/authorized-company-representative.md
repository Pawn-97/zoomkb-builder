# Authorized Company Representative

## Summary

An individual within the company whose identity documents (specifically a valid passport) and contact information are submitted as part of KYC verification. This is a distinct role from the admin performing the upload who may or may not be the same person—the UX should clarify whose documents are needed.

## Key points

- Must provide a valid passport as proof of identity (other ID forms not accepted)
- Name, contact number, and email address are required in the submission
- May be the same person as the admin or a different individual
- Their identity document is core to the verification approval
