# Contact Center Supervisor

## Summary

Can view enriched caller context information in completed engagement records, including consumer name/caller ID, transfer initiator information, and CRM ticket details. This access supports quality assurance and post-call analysis.

## Key points

- Accesses completed engagement records with full transfer context
- Can review CRM ticket information linked to calls
- Data available based on admin configuration of what to share
