# Group Member with Custom AI Companion License

## Summary

End users who can use call summary templates assigned to their group. They must have a Custom AI Companion license in addition to the standard Zoom Phone license. This dual-license requirement means license assignment visibility is important for both admins assigning templates and users wondering why templates are unavailable.

## Key points

- Must have a Custom AI Companion license to use templates
- Can only use templates assigned to their group or to 'Everyone'
- Cannot create or edit templates (admin-only)
