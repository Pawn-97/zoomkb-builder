# LTI Instructor / Admin

## Summary

Instructors and LMS admins are the only roles permitted to use Zoom Phone within LTI Pro. Their LMS role determines whether the Phone button appears in the interface. Designers must ensure unauthorized users (students) never see telephony controls, and that role-based visibility is enforced at the LMS level via NRPS.

## Key points

- Only teachers or admins of the LMS Course have Zoom Phone access in LTI Pro
- The user's Zoom account email must match the LMS login email
- Role is determined by LMS membership, not Zoom permissions
