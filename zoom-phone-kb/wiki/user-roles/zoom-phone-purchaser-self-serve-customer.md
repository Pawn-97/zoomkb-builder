# Zoom Phone Purchaser (Self-Serve Customer)

## Summary

The end customer who buys hardware through Zoom's self-serve online flow. This role is restricted to US-based customers purchasing with a new Zoom Phone license. The experience is entirely self-guided, so the interface must anticipate and answer common questions (shipping, returns, device limits) inline without requiring sales intervention.

## Key points

- Must be purchasing with a new Zoom Phone license or bundle
- Restricted to US customers with valid US street addresses (no P.O. boxes)
- Limited to 50 devices per order at checkout
- Expected to self-manage device assignment, tracking, and returns
