# AI Companion Icon in Call Toolbar

## Summary

The AI Companion feature is exposed as a dedicated icon button in the top-right corner of the call screen. Tapping it opens a modal or screen with catch-up time options, following a pattern of contextual AI actions surfaced as toolbar icons during live calls.

## Key points

- Icon placed in top-right of the push-to-talk call screen
- Single tap opens the catch-up options screen
- Consistent with other AI Companion entry points across Zoom products
