# Checklist Confirmation Gate

## Summary

Before proceeding with SMS registration, users must open and review a checklist link, then confirm they have read and understood it via a checkbox. Only after checking the box does the registration form become visible. This pattern forces deliberate acknowledgment of requirements but should balance friction with clarity.

## Key points

- Checklist link must be opened before the confirmation checkbox is meaningful
- Confirmation checkbox unlocks the registration form
- Acts as a legal/process gate to ensure requirement awareness
