# Click-to-Dial from Contact Roster

## Summary

Each contact row in LTI Pro provides a Phone icon and SMS icon that initiate calls or messages with a single click. This inline-action pattern reduces the steps needed to communicate with a course member and should be visually distinct for quick scanning.

## Key points

- Phone icon and SMS icon per contact row
- Single-click initiates the respective communication mode
- Works only after a phone number has been added to the contact
