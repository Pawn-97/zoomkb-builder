# Deactivated Selection Controls

## Summary

When prerequisites (address verification, documentation) are not met, phone number selection boxes are deactivated (grayed out). This communicates that action is blocked without explaining why — designers should pair deactivated controls with inline explanatory messages that guide admins to the resolution.

## Key points

- Selection boxes grayed out when prerequisites are incomplete
- Currently displayed without inline explanation of the blocking reason
- Common cause: unverified business address or missing documentation
- Admins must discover the Business Address & Documents page via a Related Features dropdown
