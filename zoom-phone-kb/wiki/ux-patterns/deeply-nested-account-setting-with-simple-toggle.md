# Deeply Nested Account Setting with Simple Toggle

## Summary

A binary enable/disable setting placed four navigation levels deep (Phone System Management > Company Info > Account Settings > Preference). The depth of navigation is disproportionate to the simplicity of the control (a single checkbox). Designers should evaluate whether this setting warrants a more accessible placement or whether the deep nesting is intentional due to the sensitivity of call data sharing.

## Key points

- Single checkbox controls the entire feature (no additional parameters)
- Found at path: Phone System Management > Company Info > Account Settings > Preference section
- Label hierarchy: section header ('Transferring call data to Contact Center') differs from the actual checkbox label ('Conversation summary')
