# Email Notification for Review Milestones

## Summary

Admins receive email notifications when their document review begins and when it completes. This offline notification pattern compensates for the asynchronous nature of document verification, but designers should also consider in-product status visibility to reduce reliance on email.

## Key points

- Two email notifications: at review start and at review completion
- Bridges the gap between submission and asynchronous verification result
- No in-product notification alternative mentioned
