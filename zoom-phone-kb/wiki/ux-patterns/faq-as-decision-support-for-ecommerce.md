# FAQ as Decision Support for Ecommerce

## Summary

The article uses a FAQ format to address common purchase-friction questions inline. For a self-serve ecommerce flow, designers should consider embedding these answers contextually (e.g., return policy near the purchase button, device limit in the quantity selector) rather than requiring customers to find a separate help article.

## Key points

- FAQ covers shipping, returns, device limits, tax exemption, and order tracking
- Answers should be surfaced contextually at relevant points in the purchase flow
- Reduces support tickets by preemptively addressing purchase anxiety
