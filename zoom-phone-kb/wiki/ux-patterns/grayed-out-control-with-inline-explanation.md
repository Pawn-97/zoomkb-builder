# Grayed-out Control with Inline Explanation

## Summary

When a setting is locked at a higher level, the toggle becomes grayed out with an inline note explaining the restriction. For group/site/user levels, the note indicates the setting is locked at the account level; for personal settings, it directs users to contact their admin. This inline explanation pattern avoids modal dialogs and keeps context visible.

## Key points

- Toggle rendered as disabled/grayed out, not hidden
- Inline note appears adjacent to the control explaining the restriction
- Different messaging for admins (where to change) vs. individual users (contact admin)
- Preserves visibility of the setting so users know it exists
