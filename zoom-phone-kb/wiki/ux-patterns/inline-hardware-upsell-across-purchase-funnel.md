# Inline Hardware Upsell Across Purchase Funnel

## Summary

Hardware purchase options appear at multiple touchpoints in the customer journey: the Zoom Phone pricing page (consideration), checkout (conversion), and order confirmation screen (post-purchase add-on). This pattern of surfacing hardware options throughout the funnel increases exposure without being intrusive, but requires consistent presentation across all touchpoints.

## Key points

- Hardware options surfaced on the pricing page for early consideration
- Hardware add-on available during checkout as part of the purchase flow
- Post-purchase confirmation screen offers another opportunity to add hardware
