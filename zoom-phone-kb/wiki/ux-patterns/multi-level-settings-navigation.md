# Multi-Level Settings Navigation

## Summary

The same setting (Peer to Peer Media) is accessible through four different navigation paths depending on scope: Account Settings, Groups, Company Info (Sites), and Users & Rooms. Designers should ensure consistent toggle behavior and labeling across all these paths while allowing for context-appropriate navigation.

## Key points

- Account: Account Management > Account Settings > Zoom Phone > General
- Group: User Management > Groups > [group] > Zoom Phone > General
- Site: Phone System Management > Company Info > [site] > Policy > General
- User: Phone System Management > Users & Rooms > Users > [user] > Policy > General
- Toggle label, behavior, and verification dialog are consistent across all paths
