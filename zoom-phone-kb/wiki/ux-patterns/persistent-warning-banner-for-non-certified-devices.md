# Persistent Warning Banner for Non-Certified Devices

## Summary

A persistent red warning message that remains visible to admins as long as a non-certified device exists. Designers should ensure this warning is prominent but does not block critical workflows, and consider if an acknowledgement or dismissal path is needed.

## Key points

- Red-colored warning for high visibility
- Persistent — does not auto-dismiss or time out
- Appears alongside device information in the web portal
