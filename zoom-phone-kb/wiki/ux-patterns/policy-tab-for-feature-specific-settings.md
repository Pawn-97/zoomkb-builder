# Policy Tab for Feature-Specific Settings

## Summary

At the Site, Phone User, and Common Area levels, delegation settings are located under a Policy tab rather than a general settings tab. This separation of policy controls from other configuration creates a predictable location for governance-related settings across different entity types.

## Key points

- Policy tab appears at Site, Phone User, and Common Area levels
- Separates governance/compliance settings from general configuration
- Account and Group levels use Zoom Phone tab instead, creating an inconsistency worth noting
