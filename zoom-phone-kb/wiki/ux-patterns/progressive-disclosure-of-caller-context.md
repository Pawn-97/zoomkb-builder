# Progressive Disclosure of Caller Context

## Summary

Enriched caller information follows the engagement lifecycle in three stages: incoming notification (preview before accepting), active engagement (full context during call), and completed record (post-call reference and QA). This pattern supports time-pressured agents who may not review all details before accepting.

## Key points

- Incoming notification: summary view for quick scanning before call acceptance
- Active engagement: full detail view available during the call
- Completed record: persistent record for post-call review by agents and supervisors
