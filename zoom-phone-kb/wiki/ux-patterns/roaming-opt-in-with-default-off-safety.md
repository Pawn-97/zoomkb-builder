# Roaming Opt-In with Default-Off Safety

## Summary

International roaming is deactivated by default, requiring explicit admin opt-in per user. This default-off pattern protects against unexpected charges (bill shock) and shifts the decision burden to admins who must deliberately enable roaming.

## Key points

- Default-off for financial safety
- Per-user toggle with optional date range limits
- Design consideration: surface roaming status clearly in admin overview
