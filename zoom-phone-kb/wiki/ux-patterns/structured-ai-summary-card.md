# Structured AI Summary Card

## Summary

AI-generated summaries are presented with consistent sections: Summary time period, Quick recap, Next steps, and Full summary. This structured format helps users quickly scan and locate the information they need, and provides a predictable design pattern for AI-generated content in the product.

## Key points

- Consistent four-section structure for all AI summaries
- Summary time period clarifies the temporal scope of the summary
- Quick recap provides scannable overview
- Next steps surfaces actionable items from the conversation
- Full summary provides comprehensive detail
