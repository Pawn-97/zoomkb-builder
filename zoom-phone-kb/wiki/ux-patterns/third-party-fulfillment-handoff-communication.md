# Third-Party Fulfillment Handoff Communication

## Summary

Since UC Direct handles all hardware logistics, the Zoom interface must clearly communicate the handoff at each relevant touchpoint: tracking information, technical support, returns, and address changes. This pattern requires consistent messaging that redirects customers to UC Direct while maintaining Zoom's brand experience.

## Key points

- Tracking info surfaces in Zoom Billing Portal but originates from UC Direct
- Technical support links point to UC Direct help page, not Zoom support
- Return/refund process starts from Zoom's UI but completes on UC Direct's RMA form
- Clear 'managed by our partner' messaging reduces confusion about who to contact
